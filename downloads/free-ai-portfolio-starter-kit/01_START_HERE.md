# Free AI Portfolio Starter Kit for Data Scientists

You may have done strong technical work, but explaining it publicly can still feel difficult.

This starter kit helps you take the first step toward building a clear AI/data science presence across your website, blog, and LinkedIn.

Use it to:
- clarify your positioning
- structure one project page
- improve your LinkedIn presence
- turn one blog idea into a LinkedIn post
- check whether your online presence is understandable and measurable

This is not a generic prompt pack.

It is a small practical system for AI and data science professionals who want their work to look thoughtful, credible, and clear.

## The system

Positioning → Project Page → Blog/Writing → LinkedIn → Analytics

This kit helps you move through these five steps instead of treating your website, writing, and LinkedIn as separate things.

## How to use this kit

1. Start with the portfolio checklist.
2. Use the positioning worksheet to draft your one-line direction.
3. Pick one real project.
4. Fill the project page template.
5. Use the LinkedIn checklist to improve your profile.
6. Use the prompts to turn one blog or project into a post.
7. Use the analytics checklist to track what is working.
